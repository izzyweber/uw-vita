UW VITA

Get your taxes done for free
by University of Washington students!

Eligibility

Households that make less than $66,000 are eligibile for free tax prep services. Students are IRS certified to prepare returns for domestic taxpayers as well as international students. Some limitations apply: tax returns that involve business taxes, rental income, or sale of property or stocks are not eligible for this free service.

Hours

UW VITA operates between January 15th and April 19th, 2019. We recommend coming in as soon as you have your tax forms to avoid our busiest times during late March and early April.

Mondays 4:00 pm – 7:00 pm
Wednesdays 4:00 pm – 7:00 pm
Fridays 12:00 pm – 3:00 pm

Location

We are located on the ground floor of Mackenzie Hall on the north side of UW's campus. If this location is not convenient for you, be sure to check out the full list of VITA sites in Seattle on United Way's website!
